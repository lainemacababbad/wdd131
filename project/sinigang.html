Pork Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add pork; cook until browned.
  3. Pour in water or broth; bring to a boil, then simmer until tender (about 20-30 minutes).
  4. Add chilies; season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions and enjoy with rice.
