Pork Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly or shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar (cane or white vinegar)
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1-2 cups water
  • 1-2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped green onions or hard-boiled eggs (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate pork with soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Sauté onions in oil until translucent.
  3. Add marinated pork, browning on all sides.
  4. Add reserved marinade and vinegar; do not stir for 5 minutes.
  5. Add water, peppercorns, and sugar. Simmer for 45 minutes or until tender.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with green onions or eggs if desired.

Kinilaw

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh fish (tuna or mackerel), diced
  • 1/2 cup cane vinegar
  • 1/4 cup calamansi or lime juice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1-2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/4 cup ginger, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate fish in vinegar for 10-15 minutes until it changes color.
  2. Combine marinated fish with calamansi juice, onion, tomatoes, ginger, and chilies.
  3. Season with salt and pepper; fold in coconut milk if using.
  4. Serve chilled, garnished with cilantro or green onions.

Chicken Binakol

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups coconut water (fresh preferred)
  • 1 cup young coconut meat, shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  • Chopped green onions or cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken; cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in coconut water, bring to a boil, then simmer for 20-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  4. Stir in coconut meat and chilies; season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions or cilantro, with rice.

Pork Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add pork; cook until browned.
  3. Pour in water or broth; bring to a boil, then simmer until tender (about 20-30 minutes).
  4. Add chilies; season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions and enjoy with rice.
